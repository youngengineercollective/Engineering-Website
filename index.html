Workshops K-8

Engineering workshops for curious kids.

Weekly hands-on workshops help students explore engineering through real tools and projects. Small groups and mentor guidance keep every student engaged and supported.

Why our workshops are different

  • Small groups of 6-8 kids for personal support
  • Hands-on learning from session one
  • Projects matched to age and skill level
  • Real tools, real materials, real mentoring

Workshop details

  • One workshop per week, 2 hours each
  • $199 per student each month
  • Monthly progress updates for parents
  • WhatsApp group for schedules and updates

Workshop tracks

Students choose a track and build projects that grow their confidence and skills.

Electrical & Circuits Engineering

Build circuits, alarms, and lights using batteries, switches, LEDs, and motors.

Skills: problem-solving, hands-on building

Robotics & Automation Engineering

Design and program robots with sensors, motors, and automation challenges.

Skills: coding basics, mechanical thinking, teamwork

Environmental & Sustainability Projects

Build eco-friendly solutions for renewable energy, water cleaning, and green design.

Skills: systems thinking, creativity, impact

Mechanical & Structural Engineering

Design bridges, towers, and machines, then test strength and stability.

Skills: designing, testing, iterating

Software & AI Engineering (Grades 6-8)

Create simulations, interactive projects, and explore how AI solves problems.

Skills: coding, logic, creative building

Kids meet weekly in 2-hour sessions to build, test, and improve their projects. Each cycle ends with a showcase of finished work. Parents stay informed through WhatsApp and monthly progress reports.

Our Process

Mentorship that feels like a real engineering lab.

Students learn how to think like engineers - making decisions based on evidence, documenting iterations, and presenting tradeoffs clearly.

01

Discover

Map interests, explore real-world problems, and define a project that matters.

02

Design

Sketch solutions, identify materials, and build a testing plan with clear metrics.

03

Build

Prototype in weekly sessions with feedback loops and engineering notebooks.

04

Showcase

Turn data into a story, refine visuals, and practice confident delivery.
